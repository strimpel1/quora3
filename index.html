I lived in Switzerland for one year. As a foreigner I can tell:
  • Do not flush toilet after 10 PM in Geneva. Keep it for tomorrow. Otherwise police will knock your door after 15–20 minutes.

  • You do not need a car in Geneva, it can be additional burden. Public transportation is well organized.

  • Although tap water in Switzerland is safe to drink still many people buy large bottled waters from markets for daily usage at home.

  • It is very green. Unlike many other countries they keep parks as natural as possible (without pavements).
